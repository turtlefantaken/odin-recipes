4-Ingredient Egg Avocado Toast

Home

Description

This avocado toast recipe has few ingredients and is fast to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  • 1/2 cup mashed Haas avocado
  • 1 large hard boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Smear mashed avocado on toast. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning.
  2. Top with egg. Season with salt and pepper.
